Details + FAQ

Have questions? We have answers.

What is it? A trivia night competition + fundraiser, open to everyone! Due to the COVID pandemic, the event will be fully virtual on Zoom. This time around, we'll feature head to head rounds, new minigames, and more surprises to make our seventh Fossil trivia night the best one yet!

How much does it cost?

  • Early Bird: $7/participant (if paid by 11PM 3/12).
  • Regular: $9/participant (must be paid by 11PM 4/2).
  • 100% of proceeds will be donated to the Make-A-Wish® Foundation.
    We'll have sponsors and other funds to pay for prizes and event costs to maximize your contribution.

How many participants can there be on a team? 4±1 (3-5) participants. That said, having more teammates gives your team a better advantage!

Who can participate? Anyone affiliated with Fossil Ridge (students, teachers, parents, and Fossil alumni) are welcome and encouruaged to participate. In addition, you do not need to all be in one group (i.e., all students), i.e., students can team up with alumni if desired.

Are there prizes? Yes! All teams qualify for prizes this time around. We will not give details until event night, but we will say that they're significantly better than our last event.
